About

— Websites


My Name is Jonah Checketts since I first started programming several years ago I have made dozens of websites and improved my work and hope to continue to improve it as I go. I love to build and design websites that look professional and hope that I can help you to make the website you need. In My websites I try to make them look as clean and simple as I can to help any of your customers enjoy being on your website and fall in love with your company.

— Modular


One of the most important parts of a website is making it more convenient. That is why I a make all of my websites modular on any device. Your customers can browse your website on anything from their desktop computer to their smart phone and everything in between. This is one way to move your website to the next level and help improve buisness and ease of use for your company.

— UI/UX


I prioritize ease of use for your customers when I build your website. I try to make the website look good and work good so that customers enjoy using your website. This will make them more likely to come back and be more satisfied with your company. Thus attracting more and more people helping you to grow your buisness and make everything in your company easier and more streamlined.

— Back End


Something really important that almost all websites has is backend programs. Backend programs are important to do things like the contact me pages, login systems, and any sort of data managment that you have to do on your website. I can do most of the basic things needed on websites for the backend but I am continuing to try and expand what I can do and will soon be able to do a lot more then just data managment like login systems.
